But adding to your CSS, preserveAspectRatio="none", will distort the image This will be the case when setting the height and width to 100%. Image will be distorted to extend to the size of the screen. MEET Giving the PAR, 'xmidYMin meet' will ensure the image is not distorted and placed in middle of screen. In summary, meet: preserve uniform scaling and fit entire viewBox into the viewPort. The edges of the viewBox will meet the edges of the viewport. Slice Slice will uniformly scale the viewbox to fill as much of the viewport as possible. You will see the top of the image or bottom of the image, as wide as the viewport is. Giving the PAR, 'xMaxYMin slice', will ensure the image is cut off either at the top or bottom. In summary, slice: will uniformly scale the viewBox to fill as much of the viewport as possible.
